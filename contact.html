Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about my process, timelines, and working together.

What is your typical project timeline?

Most brand identity projects take 4-8 weeks from initial briefing to final delivery. Editorial projects typically range from 2-6 weeks depending on complexity. I provide a detailed timeline at the start of each project.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes, I regularly collaborate with clients worldwide. All communication is conducted remotely via video calls, email, and collaborative platforms. Time zone differences are accommodated.

What is included in your brand identity packages?

Complete brand identity packages include primary and secondary logos, color palette, typography system, brand guidelines document, and basic stationery design. Additional assets can be added based on project needs.

How do you handle revisions?

Each project includes a specified number of revision rounds (typically 2-3) to refine the work. Additional revisions can be accommodated if needed. Clear communication ensures we stay aligned throughout the process.

What file formats will I receive?

You'll receive all source files (Adobe Creative Cloud formats), export files (PDF, JPG, PNG, SVG), and any custom fonts. All files are organized and delivered with usage instructions.

Do you offer ongoing support after project completion?

Yes, I offer ongoing support packages for clients who need regular design assistance. These can include monthly retainer agreements or hourly support as needed.
